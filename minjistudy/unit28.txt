전수씨, 늦었어요. 집에 안 가세요?
Jeonsu-sshi, it's late. You're not going home?



가요. 하지만 내일 인천으로 가요.
I'm going. But tomorrow I'm going to Incheon.



인천으로? 며칠동안 있겠어요?
To Incheon? How long are you going to stay?



아마 삼일동안.
Maybe for three days.



언제 미국으로 돌아가세요?
When are you returning to America.



모르겠어요.
I don't know.



서울에서 이삼 일동안 지내고 싶어요.
I want to spend (pass time) a few days in Seoul.



혼자 돌아가겠어요.
I'm going to return alone.



혼자에요?
Are you alone?



이삼 일 전부터 여기에 있어요.
We've been here since a few days ago.



언제 부터 여기에 있어요?
Since when have you been here?



무슨 뜻이에요?
What does it mean? (What meaning is it?)



"time"이라는 뜻이에요.
As for that, it means "time".



몇 시부터?
Since how long?



이삼 주 동안.
For a few weeks.



시간이 없어요.
I don't have time.
